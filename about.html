About Us

At 4L Studio, photography is more than a profession — It’s our passion and our art.

Founded in 2017, we have grown into one of the most sought-after photography studios, delivering over 500+ weddings and creating memories for more than 2000+ delighted clients.

With a commitment to excellence, creativity, and luxury, we specialize in capturing life’s most beautiful moments—whether it’s the elegance of a wedding, the innocence of a newborn, or the glamour of a fashion shoot.

Every frame we create is a blend of timeless artistry and modern sophistication, designed to tell stories that stay alive for generations.

Candid Photography – Unscripted moments, beautifully frozen in time.
Wedding Films – Cinematic love stories that last a lifetime.
Baby Photography – Innocence and joy in their purest form.
Fashion Photography – Style, sophistication, and glamour.
Outdoor Shoots – Natural beauty meets artistic vision.
